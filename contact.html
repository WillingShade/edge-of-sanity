Contact Policy

Tetrad Games reviews all incoming messages; however, due to development schedules and project demands, response times may vary. We prioritize inquiries related to business partnerships, publishing opportunities, media requests, and community safety concerns.

We do not accept unsolicited game pitches, design documents, or creative submissions. Any such materials sent to the studio will be deleted without review to protect both parties from potential intellectual property conflicts.

Harassment, spam, or inappropriate communication will result in blocked contact and may be reported to the appropriate platforms or authorities if necessary.
